Your fingers slip into it, as if disturbing a spiky cloud. Alas, the parts don't fit, no matter how hard you try to rearrange. You look into them, only to find a distorted image of your own self. Or, perhaps, it's the only accurate one you've ever seen. You look right, left, towards the farthest strand of your hair, to the endless abyss below. You look into yourself, at the hopeless lonely pieces that once held your delight, your joy and your naive little dreams. You are drowning, and yet you hesitate to let *it* get a hold of you, enter your lungs and stomach. In that pathetic final struggle you think about the light. The irrational illumination that filled the space, so distant yet still warm and welcoming. Could it really be.. the Sun? Could it even.. reach this far? Just to extend its hand and save *you*?
